About me

Hi there. You've stumbled upon my personal blog. I've been programming computers, mostly in JS stack, for more than 15 years now. Before that, I was programming them in amateur capacity too.

Mostly I've learned computers would probably do better without the humans, if they could (they say they will now, soon, *maybe*).

For the last 8 years I've been working within the React ecosystem, while working as an employee of a fintech startup, and for the last 4 - at a company developing employee management software.

But now that I've started appreciating the thinking side more than the coding one, I'll instead try to just write about what I like and think about, both as a person and a developer. Hopefully there will be at least something on the blog!

I enjoy drawing childish illustrations, running, tinkering with arduinos, reading books, fixing up my old inherited farm house. Maybe some of that will appear here.

P.S. I'm from Lithuania, not an english speaker, and I try to keep this space as personal as possible, meaning I will not use ML tools to edit the text, and you can expect me to talk like this if you ever meet me. Yup.
